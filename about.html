Little bit about me..

I think it all started pre-highschool where I fell in love with sports and video games. At first I wanted to develop and make video games but then I quickly realised that I enjoy playing them instead. Once I reached highschool I became inveloped with my first laptop and fell in love with learning everything about it. I started out burning music cd's and dvd's making a little cash on the side. As I began to learn more about computers new technologies kept surfacing especially cellphones. From my freshman year in highschool I distintly remeber the "Motorola razr" as the hottest flip phone out, by my senior year I had an "LG Optimus L70" my first smart phone. So in a little as 4 years we came from T9-texting to the Swipe keyboard where you don't need to lift a finger to type a word. As of today we can now say a simple command such as "Ok Google" and send messages within a few seconds without lifting a finger. I really do appreciate how technology has made our lives much easier especially communication but with every positive there is surely a negative.
